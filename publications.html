Research Interests:

  • The formation and distribution of brown dwarfs in the local galaxy.
  • The orbital properties of planets as a function of host star characteristics.
  • The internal processes of intrinsically variable stars and how that relates to their environment.
  • Galactic formation history and the structure of the galactic halo.
  • Dwarf spheroidal galaxies and their relationship to the formation history of the Local Group.
  • Development of software for the detection and characterization of large numbers of variable objects for surveys.

Research Collaboration

  • Observing and Operations Manager for the SDSS-III MARVELS survey.
  • Architect status in SDSS-III MARVELS collabortation
  • Member of the LSST Stars, Milky Way & Local Volume collaboration
  • Member of the TESS initial target selection team at Vanderbilt

Research Experience Observing The Following:

  • Apache Point 2.5m telescope 2008 - 2011 Purpose: Observing nearby stars for planets as part of the SDSS-III MARVELS Survey.
  • Michigan State University 24 inch telescope 2002 - 2008 Purpose: Photometry of Type-II Cepheid lightcurves in globular clusters and RR Lyrae lightcurves in the g, r, and i.
  • Kitt Peak YALO 0.9 m with the S2KB imager Nov 2005; Mar, Apr, Oct 2007 Purpose: Stellar parameter determination using UBV and BVRI photometry.
  • SOAR 4 m telescope with the OSIRIS infrared spectrograph Oct 2007 Purpose: Determining the oxygen abundance of carbon enhanced metal poor stars.
  • Blanco 4 m telescope with the RC spectrograph Aug/Sept 2007 Purpose: Determination of Radial Velocities and Metallicities for halo SDSS RR Lyrae stars
  • SOAR 4 m telescope with the SOAR Optical Imager Feb-Mar 2006; Mar, Apr, May, Jun 2007 Purpose: Photometric variable surveys of SDSS dwarf spheroidals and of globular clusters.
  • CTIO 0.9 m with the QUAD CCD May 2005 Purpose: Stellar parameter determination using UBV and BVRI photometry.
  • Blanco 4 m telescope with the Mosaic imager Nov 2004 Purpose: Variable star survey of the Fornax Dwarf Spheroidal galaxy.
